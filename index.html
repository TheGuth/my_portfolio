ABOUT ME

I'm James, a full stack developer in Indianapolis, Indiana. I love to learn, whether it's a skill like installing hardwood floors in my living room or trying out a new technology such as React-Native.

When I'm not learning something new, I enjoy board games, video games, and hanging out with my friends. However, if you think you can beat me in some good ole Nintendo 64 games such as, Smash or Mario Cart, we will need to talk. Other than that, I'm a pretty chill guy who loves going on walks with my wife and playing with my German shepherd (Ember).

Before I got into programming, I was a Social Studies Teacher at Peru Jr. High School, teaching 7th and 8th grade. I also coached high school tennis and Jr. High Track, was Geography Bowl Leader, National Jr. Honor Society Co-Leader, and involved with many other school related activities. As I taught, I found myself often trying to create and build my own resources. Eventually I found that I didn't have the necessary coding skills to build interactive and useful resources. Fast forward to now, I have built multiple full-stack applications, and I'm continuing to learn and improve my current skills. The technology that I'm most interested in learning next is GraphQL.
